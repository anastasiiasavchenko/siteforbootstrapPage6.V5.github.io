Made in Italy

In our company, the most modern technology and traditional craftsmanship come together. We only use trained specialist personnel. While the most advanced computer-assisted machines process fine and exclusive materials and we produce special components ourselves using our own 3D printer, the specialists in our workshop also work with traditional hand tools to create the finest interior for you. Precision is one of our passions.

  • What began in 1905 as a carpentry shop in a small village has developed into a globally active joinery producing furniture and high-quality interiors.
  • Much has changed since then.
  • What has remained, however, is the love for our basic material wood, the joy in the finest details, the urge for the highest quality and precision and the attachment to our home in the Münsterland.

For us, the highest quality without compromise is the first priority in our actions, followed by a love of the finest, first-class materials, excellent service, a craftsmanship that draws on exclusive knowledge and many years of experience, and a reliable service that guarantees our customers’ satisfaction. We want to relieve interior designers and designers of as much work as possible so that they can concentrate fully on the ideas for their customers’ living spaces. We are always true to our word. Agreements and promises are strictly adhered to. We treat our partners and employees with respect and honesty. If there are changes, we react flexibly to ensure a smooth process. In addition to quality, reliability, punctuality and precision, we also focus on skill and knowledge down to the smallest detail. In doing so, we bundle our strengths and attach great importance to the further development of technological craftsmanship and skills. Our efforts are focused on your individual wishes, which we always strive to implement with the utmost precision.

Quality

We want you to enjoy our products for a long time. For this reason we have set high standards for our designs, the materials used and their processing. Thus our work convinces in every detail!

Experience

As a family-run company with more than 115 years of experience, we offer our customers impressive technical expertise, which enables us to realise even unusual projects.

Team spirit

We believe that we can only achieve more as a team. With the expertise of more than 100 employees from various fields, we can develop and implement complex, demanding projects.

Reliability

Trust is an important cornerstone of our work – within the team as well as in cooperation with partners and with our customers. With us, you can always rely on high quality and individual service.

Tradition & Innovation

For our successful work it is equally important to preserve the traditional achievements of the craft and to discover and use the advantages of progress again and again.

Sustainabilty

The protection of natural resources is important to us. In order to do our bit for nature conservation, we use, for example, wood from responsibly managed forests and environmentally friendly varnishes.
